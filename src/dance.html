Dance Program

Our dance program offers a vibrant and inclusive community where dancers of all ages and skill levels can thrive. With a diverse range of classes including Mini's for our youngest movers, Youth for budding talents, and dynamic offerings in Jazz and Hip-Hop, we provide a comprehensive dance education that fosters technique, creativity, and confidence. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to nurturing each dancer's unique abilities while instilling a passion for movement and expression. Whether it's mastering a new step, refining technique, or unleashing creativity on the dance floor, our program is designed to inspire and empower dancers to reach their full potential while building lasting friendships and memories along the way. Join us and let your dance journey begin!

Mini's Dance

Designed for the youngest dancers, Mini's classes offer a playful introduction to movement, rhythm, and coordination. Through fun and imaginative activities, children explore basic dance techniques while fostering a love for dance in a nurturing environment.

Youth Dance

Tailored for young dancers, Youth classes focus on developing technique, strength, and flexibility while encouraging creativity and self-expression. With an emphasis on proper form and alignment, dancers explore various dance styles and choreography in a supportive atmosphere.

Jazz

Jazz classes blend elements of ballet, modern dance, and African rhythms to create a dynamic and expressive style of dance. Dancers learn a wide range of movements, from sharp isolations to fluid transitions, set to upbeat music. With an emphasis on rhythm, style, and musicality, jazz classes offer dancers the opportunity to unleash their energy and individuality.

Hip Hop

Hip-hop classes offer a high-energy and urban-inspired style of dance, incorporating elements of street dance, popping, locking, and breaking. Dancers learn choreography to the latest hip-hop music while focusing on precision, rhythm, and personal style. With an emphasis on groove and attitude, hip-hop classes provide a fun and engaging workout for dancers of all levels.
